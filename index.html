Xinchen Wan

Room 3661, HKUST

Clear Water Bay

Kowloon, Hong Kong SAR, China

I am a Ph.D candidate in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Kai Chen. Before that, I received my B.Eng in Computer Science in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2018.

My research interests include distributed machine learning system, data center networking, and hardware acceleration.
